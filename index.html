Features

Everything you need to present

Freehand Drawing

Pen with trackpad pressure sensitivity, smooth Bézier curves, and a laser pointer that fades over time.

■

Shapes & Arrows

Rectangles, ellipses, lines, and arrows — draw perfect shapes on top of any application in any mode.

▬

Highlighter

Transparent highlighter strokes with multiply blend mode. Perfect for emphasizing text and code.

◉

Cursor Highlight

A glowing ring follows your cursor everywhere. Click-ripple animations help your audience track your pointer.

◐

Spotlight

Dim the entire screen and spotlight a specific area. Smooth radial gradient edges keep it looking polished.

Zoom Lens

A floating circular magnifier captures the screen near your cursor. Adjustable magnification from 1.5x to 5x.

Whiteboard Mode

Full-screen canvas in white, black, green, or blue. Draw freely as on a real whiteboard during lessons.
