Frequently Asked Questions

Does FnMark work with Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams?

Yes. FnMark draws on top of your screen, so anything you annotate is visible to people watching your shared screen — regardless of the meeting platform.

Do I need to grant Screen Recording permission?

Only if you use the Zoom Lens or Export-as-PNG features. Basic annotation works without it. macOS will prompt you the first time you use those features.

Does FnMark collect any data?

No. FnMark runs entirely on your Mac. There is no telemetry, no analytics, no internet connection at all.

Can I customize the keyboard shortcuts?

Yes. Every shortcut is customizable in Settings → Shortcuts, including the "interactive mode" key (default Fn) used to enable drawing.

Does it work on multiple monitors?

Yes. FnMark creates an overlay on every connected display.

What macOS version do I need?

macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. Apple Silicon and Intel Macs are both supported.

Is it a one-time purchase?

Yes. One purchase, free updates forever. No subscriptions.
